Personal Profile

A self-motivated and passionate Civil Engineer looking to pursue a Master's in Structural Engineering. Currently a researcher and Sub-Instructor for technical students at Janajagriti Secondary School, Dhading, Nepal. I have a palpable desire to research sustainable building materials, sustainable concrete, and earthquake-resistant buildings.

I am particularly interested in supplementary cementitious materials like bamboo leaves, bamboo flakes, rice husk, water hyacinth, and studying their strength, durability properties while used in varying mix proportions. I am also passionate about anything that focuses on sustainability in structures and materials.
